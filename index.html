About

Dealmaker in Training. India-Focused by Design.

I am completing an MSc in Private Equity, Venture Capital & Financial Technology at UCL, graduating August 2026. I hold all three CFA levels cleared with a Private Markets specialisation, and a BAF degree from the University of Mumbai with a 9.08 CGPA.

My commercial foundation is grounded in real deal experience — independently originating and closing commodity trade transactions at Neeta International, institutional equity research at Morningstar India, and wealth management across a two-and-a-half-year client-facing practice. I am not theorising about markets. I have traded in them.

My focus is front-office dealmaking across Mumbai and the Gulf — PE, IB, and growth capital in the markets that matter most for the next decade of Indian capital formation.
